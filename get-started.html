How To Get Involved

☆ If you are a UM-Dearborn student, join us on VictorsLink!

☆ Join our Discord to meet our members, have fun conversations, and receive announcements!

☆ Follow our instagram and other social media for updates, news, and other fun content!

☆ If you would like to receive monthly emails containing updates, important links, and event reminders, sign up for our newsletter!

☆ Fill out the membership form and pay dues for official member benefits, which includes the AKA/Family Program, the privilege to vote in elections, being included in housing/travel plans for out-of-town trips, receiving a Filipino stole for graduation, a yearly SAFA member shirt, and more!
